Life of Pie Website Redesign

Description

For this project, my group and I were tasked with creating a mobile app for commercialization aimed at addressing everyday challenges. We chose to tackle the issue of saving time and money during grocery shopping.


Our app, named Smart Cart, provides a convenient solution by allowing users to choose between saving money and going to the nearest location that has their desired products in stock. Additionally, the app features a balance function, allowing users to optimize for both location and savings. It's important to note that this app is currently in the design phase and is functional only through our Figma prototype. I've highlighted the specific pages I personally worked on.
